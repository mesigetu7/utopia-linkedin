The SterKeys project started with stairs.

A luxury residential client. Specific vision. Wanted something that felt custom, not catalog. The brief was clear: a staircase that matched the quality of the rest of the interior.

We cast it in UHPC. The geometry they wanted — the profile, the edge detail, the finish — things that would have required compromises in conventional concrete came out exactly as designed.

That's the thing about working with a Pioneer. They don't need to be convinced. They have a clear picture of what they want and they're willing to invest to get there. The back-and-forth isn't about whether the material can do it. It's about how to push it further.

Now the same client wants us to do the flooring.

And we're designing a custom bench as a gift — a piece that will sit in the space and quietly do the work of showing every visitor what this material is capable of.

Every Pioneer creates Validators. Every finished project is the argument the next client needs to see.
