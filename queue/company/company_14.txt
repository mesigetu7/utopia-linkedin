The mould defines everything.

With GFRC, any surface texture can be embedded into the cast. Stone grain. Timber plank. Woven pattern. Sharp geometric reveals. Smooth as glass.

The surface is not a coating applied after manufacture — it is formed during casting. It does not peel, fade, or require repainting.

For exterior facades in Ethiopia's climate — intense UV, seasonal rain, temperature variation — this is not a cosmetic choice. It is a durability decision.

We cast GFRC facade panels with custom surface profiles at our facility in Addis Ababa. Every panel is made to specification.
