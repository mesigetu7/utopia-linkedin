Before UHPC, a concrete bench required significant mass to be structurally sound.

Heavy base. Thick seat. Visible reinforcement or bulky support. The geometry was driven by the material's limitations.

UHPC benches can be cast as thin-shell forms — minimal thickness, clean profiles, cantilevered sections, geometric edges. They look designed. Because they are designed — not engineered around material constraints.

For public plazas, corporate campuses, hotel terraces, and airport lounges, the bench is often a detail that communicates the quality of the whole environment.

We manufacture UHPC benches and site furniture in Ethiopia, to custom specifications.
