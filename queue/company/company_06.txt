The numbers behind UHPC are worth understanding.

Compressive strength: 150–200 MPa (vs 25–40 MPa for standard concrete).
Flexural strength: 15–40 MPa (vs 3–5 MPa for standard concrete).
Water absorption: near zero.
Service life: 75–100 years with minimal maintenance.

These are not marketing claims. They are material properties — the result of a mix design that eliminates capillary porosity almost entirely.

For Ethiopia's large-scale infrastructure, this means structures that perform across decades of wet seasons, temperature swings, and high traffic loads — without the maintenance cycle that conventional concrete requires.
