A column does not have to be round.

It does not have to be square, either. With UHPC, a structural or decorative column can be any cross-section the design requires — fluted, tapered, organically curved, or faceted.

The material casts precisely and holds the profile without additional finishing work. The result integrates with the architectural language of the building rather than working against it.

For lobby spaces, hotel entrances, commercial atriums, and public civic buildings, the column detail is often the element that defines whether the architecture reads as ordinary or exceptional.

We produce custom column forms in UHPC at our Addis Ababa facility.
