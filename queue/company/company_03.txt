Glass Fibre Reinforced Concrete weighs 75% less than standard concrete.

Same visual presence. Same weather resistance. A fraction of the structural load.

For facade panels, this changes the entire cost model. Lighter cladding means lighter substructure. Lighter substructure means a different foundation requirement. At scale, the savings compound.

GFRC also holds complex geometry that heavy precast cannot. Curves, thin edges, deep reveals, intricate surface textures — the material captures the detail because it is cast, not poured.

We produce GFRC in Ethiopia. Locally made, quality-controlled, delivered to site.
