Every facade tells the story of the material that built it.

Rough aggregate, exposed. Smooth as polished stone. Textured like woven fabric. Deep shadow lines cast by geometric reveals.

With GFRC, the surface is not applied — it is part of the cast. The texture, the color, the profile: all of it locked in during manufacturing, not added after.

For architects designing landmark buildings in Addis Ababa, this is not a small detail. The facade is the building's public face. It deserves a material that holds its quality over decades of sun and rain.

We produce GFRC facade systems in Ethiopia. Custom textures, custom colors, any geometry your design requires.
