Addis Ababa is one of the fastest-growing cities in Africa.

The construction happening now — in Bole, CMC, Gerji, Kazanchis — is defining what this city looks like for the next generation.

Most of it is being built with conventional concrete, standard block, and imported cladding systems that require regular maintenance in a climate that is hard on materials.

We believe Ethiopian construction deserves better specifications. Not for luxury only — for every project where the developer wants the building to perform over time, not just at handover.

GFRC and UHPC are not aspirational materials. They are practical ones. The only question is whether Ethiopian builders are ready to specify what actually performs.

We are ready when you are.
